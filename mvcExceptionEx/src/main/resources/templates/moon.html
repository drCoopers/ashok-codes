About the Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite and is the fifth-largest satellite in the Solar System. It is approximately 1/6th the size of Earth and has a gravitational influence that causes tides.

Key Facts

  • Diameter: 3,474 kilometers (2,159 miles)
  • Distance from Earth: Approximately 384,400 kilometers (238,855 miles)
  • Surface Features: Craters, mountains, and maria (dark plains)
  • Orbit: Completes one orbit around Earth in about 27.3 days
